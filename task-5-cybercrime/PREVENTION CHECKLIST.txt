Stay Safe Online – Checklist for College Students in India

✅ You should use unique passwords for every single account and these passwords should have at least 12 characters with symbols and numbers in them.

✅ It is an idea to enable Two-Factor Authentication on your email, social media and banking apps to stay safe online.

✅ Never click on links or open attachments that you get via email, SMS or WhatsApp if they look suspicious to you.

✅ You should avoid using Wi-Fi for banking making UPI payments or logging into accounts that have sensitive information.

✅ Keep your devices updated with the security patches and antivirus software to protect them from viruses.

✅ Do not share much personal information like your phone number, address or college ID on social media platforms.

✅ Always verify job or internship offers before you share any documents because there are scams that target students, like you.

💰 UPI and Financial Safety Tips

💳 Always check the name of the person you are sending money to before you confirm any UPI payment to make sure it is the person.

💳 Never approve collect requests unless you are absolutely sure because scammers often try to misuse this feature.

💳 Do not share your OTP, UPI PIN or banking details with anyone even if they claim to be calling from your bank.
